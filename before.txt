School Management Software

Advantages:
- Excel File Generation: This software offers the advantage of generating Excel files of student records based on user-defined conditions, providing ease of data management and analysis.
- Photo Storage: Users can save photos of students within the application, allowing for better identification and personalization of student profiles.
- Real-Time Database Connectivity: The software connects seamlessly with a real-time database, ensuring that all information is up-to-date and readily accessible.

Disadvantages:
- User Interface: The placement of labels and buttons within the software interface may not adapt well to different window sizes on computers, potentially leading to a less intuitive user experience.
